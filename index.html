CHRISTIAN GRAY

Welcome! I'm Christian Gray, a 20-year-old music compositions major at Boise State University, originally from Idaho Falls. With a decade of experience in guitar playing and six years in music composition, I'm passionate about crafting melodies and sharing my expertise. Explore my compositions, listen to my guitar work, and consider joining me for personalized lessons. Additionally, if you're in need of original music for your projects, I offer bespoke compositions tailored to your needs.

My Music

These various themed songs showcase not only my passion and versatility but also my collaborative skills within the expansive world of music.

  • Trappist-G

    This musical composition was a collaborative endeavor between our team and the physics department at BSU, focusing on orchestrating the harmonies of plant Tappist-G.

  • The Final Frontier

    This vibrant musical piece was a delightful collaborative effort with the Gaming, Interactive, Mobile Media department at BSU, earning first place in Art for a Game Jam competition.

  • Hiding In The Fog

    The atmospheric and eerie notes of this piece were integrated into another collaborative project with the GIMM department to illustrate the jam game theme of cosmic horror.

  • Fight or Die

    Alongside the previous piece, this song serves as a guide through the world of game development and cosmic horror, seamlessly blending thematic elements to offer an immersive experience.

  • Among The Stars

    This solo piano piece embodies soul and passion, crafted for one of my diverse composition projects.

  • Jungle Lo-Fi

    This Lo-Fi calming song was featured in another GIMM jungle-themed project, illustrating the serene yet captivating atmosphere of the wild jungle.
